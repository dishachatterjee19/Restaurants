About Our Restaurent

We Create Emotions

Miss Disha, has been cooking Exotic Indian dishes for several years.

Disha started cooking as a hobby. She enjoyed preparing delicious food for her friends and family. Impressed by her professional touch and unique taste, many of her friends and others who enjoyed her talent in cooking asked her to open a restaurant. Her passion for inventing new recipes and serving to others finally created the Disha's Restaurant.

So come join us for a dinner or lunch. Disha's restaurant's cozy atmosphere, great tasting food and selected drinks will surely make you feel at home! In addition to an indoor dinning area, the restaurant also has a spacious wooden deck to enjoy our dishes while experiencing the beauty of outdoors.
